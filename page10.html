GROTTO OF OUR LADY OF LOURDES

SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, BULACAN

INFORMATION

Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes is located
at San Jose Del Monte Bulacan. The church
comes with a shrine behind that shows the
Holy Water Spring and a lot of clothing and
food stalls right at the foot of the church.

FUN FACT

Fun Fact about this church is this is a replica of the Grotto Church in France. Although the church seems old inside, the beauty remains deep within the place as you explore around the Grotto.

OUR REVIEW

Mass starts at 9am in the morning as it goes on within the day like other churches. Our review on this church is lovely and it will surely be worth the visit to everyone who wishes to go here,

BITBIT BRIDGE / HILLTOP VIEW

NORZAGARAY, BULACAN

INFORMATION

BitBit Bridge or mostly known as the Hilltop view is located at Norzagaray, Bulacan before the entrance on the way to Angat Dam. This bridge is known for it's beautiful view down the riverbank and cold breeze.

FUN FACT

Fun Fact about this place is that this is what they call the 'Baguio' place of Bulacan because of the fresh and cold breeze and how high the trip to this bridge is. Also, the river below this bridge came from the water of Angat Dam.

OUR REVIEW

The river below BitBit Bridge is open for people to swim and have fun as there is also this rock formation in the middle of the river. There are also mini cottages for people to stay in and a wide view of the place. For us, BitBit Bridge is a wonderful place to stay to relax.

STA. ISABEL CHURCH

STA. ISABEL, MALOLOS, BULACAN

INFORMATION

Sta. Isabel Church is located near Barangay San Pablo, and in Barangay Sta. Isabel, Malolos, Bulacan. This church is known for it's beautiful interior.

FUN FACT

Fun Fact about Sta. Isabel Parish Church is this is one of the first churches built and one of the oldest ones in Bulacan.

OUR REVIEW

Our review on this place is very pretty. It is worth the visit and it does follow the mass duties. The place is neat and well decorated mostly during Christmas season. It is beautiful, overall.

KALAYAAN TREE

CATHEDRAL CHURCH, MALOLOS BAYAN, BULACAN

INFORMATION

Kalayaan Tree is located in the Cathedral Church of Malolos Bayan, Bulacan. This will be seen infront of the church with a huge tree and you will notice statues having a meeting on this particular tree.

FUN FACT

Fun Fact about this Kalayaan Tree on why is this so special is because it is said that this tree is planted by the one and only, Aguinaldo and bloomed to be a huge tree where Aguinaldo and the others will always have a meeting or discussion under this tree which they got the name, 'Kalayaan' from.

OUR REVIEW

Our review of this place is that, this is not known in the place and only a few citizens know the importance of this tree. As we grew up in Malolos, we just knew the purpose of these statues and why are they built under this tree which is a huge significance to the church itself and Malolos, Bulacan.

BARASOAIN CHURCH

MALOLOS, BULACAN

INFORMATION

Barasoain Church is located in Malolos, Bulacan which is very known to be a tourist spot because this is where Aguinaldo shifted the capital from Manila to Malolos where a formal inauguration is built of the First Philippine Republic.

FUN FACT

Fun Fact about Barasoain Church is this has become a landmark of Malolos and is visited by a lot of famous people such as Joseph Estrada and many more which received a lot of history of the Philippines.

OUR REVIEW

Our review of Barasoain Church is beautiful. There is a elegant museum of saints inside the church and a little park inside. Also, you will see the history of the church in the museum upstairs containing wax figures of Aguinaldo and his men. All in all, Barasoain Church is a must-see tourist spot and will never waste your time and fun exploring Bulacan.
